Artist Bio:

Qaqamba, a rising Afro jazz artist, was born in EC eNgcobo but now resides in CPT eMfuleni. As a community activist, songwriter, singer, and poet, drawing inspiration from social injustices, using her music to raise awareness and promote communal healing.

Qaqamba has been a backup singer for artists such as Mahalia Buchanan and has opened stages for big artists like Zolani Mahola.

Qaqamba has also performed at the Msaki & Friends event hosted at Cabo Beach Club by Anything Goes. She got to share the stage with Msaki, Anatii, Chronos, Mpho.wav and KaryendaSoul.

A Bridges for Music Academy graduate, she gained expertise in music production, business, and mindfulness, releasing singles like "Zizinja" and "Maqondana".

Qaqamba, also a Digital Marketing specialist for a company called Comarketers, combines her skills to maximize her presence in the music industry.

Qaqamba has also been nominated for the Basadi in Music Awards 2024 for the Jazz Artist of the year category. Not only that, this legendary performer got to set foot on the BIMA Stage at Joburg Theatre paying tribute to Lebo Mathosa with “I love Music” and Zahara with “Xabendinomama”. Qaqamba plans to expand her fan base beyond her neighborhood.
