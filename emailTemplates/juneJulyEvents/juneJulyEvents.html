09 June - 06 July: Where Did The Stories Go (Part 1)

'Where Did The Stories Go?' - Part 1

'What happened to the Epics?
That could place into script
The slivers of the purity
And destructions our flesh is so readily capable.
Our existence made immortal.
Oh, what ever happened to the Epics?'

This excerpt from the poem 'The Epics' is central to the conceptualisation of the exhibition installed at Dweba Art & Café. It looks beyond a 30 second iteration of narrative and asks the viewer to ponder the longing for enduring story telling.

These artists reflect on stories they hold dear to themselves, and unveil them for the public.
