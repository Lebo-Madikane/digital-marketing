Refiloe Mnisi: Solo Show(Inside = Outside)

We are proud to present Refiloe Mnisi's Solo Show — an intimate unveiling of his latest visual stories. Refiloe brings raw emotion, vibrant colour, and deeply expressive layers that speak to both the present and ancestral memory.

Join us for an evening of art, conversation, and culture. This marks Refiloe’s first solo showcase since joining the Dweba Art Residency Programme, and we cannot wait to share this moment with our growing creative community.

Now Showing: Golden Kings Code

Exhibition by Kevin Wurffel | May – 28 June 2025


Golden Kings Code is a body of work that explores identity, resilience and the untold stories of African Kingship through vibrant colour, intricate symbolism, and powerful visual language.

Running through to 28 June 2025, this thought-provoking body of work invites viewers to decode stories of legacy and memory — layered in gold, grit, and grace.
